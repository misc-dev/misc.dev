2020-04-25 18:43

Microphone check, one two, one two!

  • M: Management
  • I: Information Studies (and Interface/Interaction Design)
  • S: Systems Thinking/Theory
  • C: Complexity
  • and of course, software development

Hence, again, the name misc.dev.
